About Us

Mission Statement

To promote and empower the LGBT Community and its allies through information, education, advocacy and support.

History

1978 - Gay Community Services is formed (GCS). GCS was predominantly formed as a social group for safe space meet-ups and was incorporated as a nonprofit on November 27, 1978.

1979 - Gay Men's Hotline formed. The number was 407-THE-GAYS and was staffed by volunteers. Gay men could call for referrals, mental health counseling, and up to date information on GCS meet-ups.

Free STI Testing
(Sexually Transmitted Infection)

Monday - Friday
9:00 am - 4:00 pm
Saturday
10:00 am - 1:45 pm



Photo ID required
No appointment needed
Must be age 13 years or older
No parent or guardian necessary
Repeat testing allowed after 3 months
No testing within 3 days of menstruation
Results available within 2 weeks (results provided in-person ONLY with photo ID)

STI Testing is done in partnership with the State of Florida’s Department of Health. Testing is done at our Health & Human Services Building-1200 Hillcrest Street Suite 102, Orlando, FL 32803.

Older Wiser Learners

An in-person group for LGBTQ+ older adults. Weekly meetings include Game Day & Senior Social, Grief & Loss Support Group, and monthly Lunch & Learns. Join our Older Wiser Learners Facebook Group at: https://www.facebook.com/groups/OwlsCentralFlorida. For more information on the OWL Program or other senior services, email info@thecenterorlando.org.

Q Chat Space for Youth

Find and give support, have fun, connect around shared interests, and get good information. Chat with like-minded peers in live chats designed for you and by you, facilitated by folks who care. Facilitators are people who work at LGBTQ+ centers across the US.
