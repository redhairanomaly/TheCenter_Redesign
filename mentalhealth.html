What We Do


We are glad you are here, and you have already taken the hardest step in the counseling process, deciding that you should look for counseling. There are many reasons why people seek counseling. They may have a problem they want to get help with, desire personal growth, or may just need someone to talk to. Whatever the reason, our dedicated and passionate counselors are ready to work with individuals, couples, and/or families in a LGBTQ+ sensitive environment.


Our counselors specialize in working with LGBTQ+ related issues. Issues such as coming out, transitioning, and all LGBTQ+ related issues in between. We know that the reason that you have chosen our counseling program is because our counselors are LGBTQ+ competent. They understand the issues that are unique to our community as well as common mental health concerns.
